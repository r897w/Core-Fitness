Exercises Guide

Learn how to use gym equipment correctly and safely

Scroll to see more exercises →

Bench Press

Exercise: Barbell bench press

Instruction: Lower the bar to your chest and press up with control.

Safety Tip: Use a spotter and keep your wrists straight.

Chest Fly

Exercise: Machine chest fly

Instruction: Bring the handles together slowly while keeping your elbows slightly bent.

Safety Tip: Do not overstretch your shoulders.

Lat Pulldown

Exercise: Lat pulldown

Instruction: Pull the bar toward your upper chest with controlled movement.

Safety Tip: Do not pull the bar behind your neck.

Pull-Up

Exercise: Pull-ups

Instruction: Pull your body up until your chin reaches the bar.

Safety Tip: Avoid swinging your body.

Barbell Row

Exercise: Bent-over row

Instruction: Keep your back straight and pull the bar toward your torso.

Safety Tip: Avoid rounding your lower back.

Incline Bench

Exercise: Incline chest press

Instruction: Press the weight upward while keeping your shoulders stable.

Safety Tip: Keep your back supported on the bench.

Squat Rack

Exercise: Barbell squat

Instruction: Lower your body with control while keeping your chest up.

Safety Tip: Use safety bars and keep proper form.

Leg Press

Exercise: Leg press push

Instruction: Push the platform away using your legs with controlled movement.

Safety Tip: Do not lock your knees.

Barbell RDL

Exercise: Romanian deadlift

Instruction: Hinge at your hips and lower the bar while keeping your back straight.

Safety Tip: Keep the bar close to your legs.

Calf Press

Exercise: Calf press

Instruction: Push through your toes and slowly lower your heels.

Safety Tip: Move slowly and avoid bouncing.

Cable Crunch

Exercise: Cable crunch

Instruction: Crunch downward using your abs, not your arms.

Safety Tip: Keep the movement controlled.

Treadmill

Exercise: Incline walk or running

Instruction: Start slow and increase speed or incline gradually.

Safety Tip: Keep your posture straight and avoid looking down.

Elliptical

Exercise: Elliptical cardio

Instruction: Push and pull smoothly while keeping your body balanced.

Safety Tip: Keep your feet flat and avoid leaning too much.

Rowing Machine

Exercise: Rowing

Instruction: Push with your legs, then pull with your arms.

Safety Tip: Keep your back straight and avoid slouching.

StairMaster

Exercise: Stair climbing

Instruction: Step steadily and keep your core engaged.

Safety Tip: Do not lean heavily on the handles.
