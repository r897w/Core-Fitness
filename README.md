# Core-Fitness

## Project Idea
Core Fitness is a web application developed for gym beginners to help them understand how to properly use gym equipment and follow the right workout routine. Core Fitness offers guidance on different types of exercises, targeted muscles, and training goals: bulking, cutting, and maintenance.

## Business Problem
Many gym users don’t know how to properly use gym equipment or which machines target specific muscle groups. They may also feel confused when choosing the most suitable exercises. Additionally, they don’t know what weight to use based on their goals, which may cause users to have slow progress or potentially injure themselves through their training.

## Proposed Solution
Core Fitness provides a simple and organized platform where users can explore gym equipment, learn how to perform exercises correctly, and follow structured workout plans. The application also helps users choose their fitness goal (bulking, cutting, maintenance, or athletic performance) and guides them in selecting appropriate weights based on their body condition and fitness level.

## Application Sections

### Goal Guide (Bulking / Cutting / Maintenance / Athlete + InBody)

- recommended reps
- intensity level
- weight level
- body fat percentage
- muscle mass

Goal Types:
- Maintenance (Balanced Body)
- Cutting (Fat Loss)
- Bulking (Muscle Gain)
- Athlete / Performance (Strength and Endurance)
